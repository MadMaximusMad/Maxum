# Activity Layer

**Activity is the primary driver of system expansion.**

Activity includes all interactions that utilize liquidity. This includes trading, asset exchange, and application-level transactions.

Activity is measurable as throughput. Throughput determines the volume of fees generated and therefore the rate at which capital is accumulated.

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Key dependency**

The system expands only when activity is sustained. Without sustained activity, the system cannot generate revenue or expand capital capacity.
{% endhint %}

Higher activity increases fee generation, which increases capital formation and expands the balance sheet.

Activity emerges from [Market Layer](market-layer.md), is monetized in [Revenue Layer](revenue-layer.md), and is the core growth condition summarized in [System Loop](../system/system-loop.md).
