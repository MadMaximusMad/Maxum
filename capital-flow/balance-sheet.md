# Balance Sheet

**The balance sheet is the central state of the system.**

It aggregates all accumulated capital and defines three critical constraints: circulating supply limits through RFV, total deployable capital, and system stability capacity.

As revenue accumulates, the balance sheet expands. This expansion increases the system’s ability to deploy liquidity, support markets, and absorb volatility.

The balance sheet is not passive storage. It is the mechanism through which the system maintains coherence between capital, supply, and liquidity.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Interpretation**

The balance sheet is the system’s state, not a passive reserve.
{% endhint %}

The balance sheet receives inflow from [Revenue Layer](revenue-layer.md), constrains [Supply Model](../mechanisms/README.md), and funds [Capital Deployment](capital-deployment.md).
