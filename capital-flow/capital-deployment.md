# Capital Deployment

**Capital deployment converts stored value into productive infrastructure.**

Capital is deployed from the balance sheet into liquidity across active markets. This process is continuous and adaptive, responding to system conditions and activity levels.

As capital accumulates, liquidity expands. Increased liquidity deepens markets and improves execution conditions, enabling larger and more frequent transactions.

Capital is not deployed uniformly. It is allocated where it can generate the highest level of activity and throughput.

{% hint style="success" %}
**System property**

Capital becomes productive only when deployed as liquidity.
{% endhint %}

Liquidity therefore represents capital in motion. It is the mechanism through which stored value becomes productive.

Deployment draws directly from [Balance Sheet](balance-sheet.md), becomes active in [Market Layer](market-layer.md), and is reinforced by the dynamics described in [Liquidity System](../mechanisms/liquidity-system.md).
