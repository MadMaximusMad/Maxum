# Revenue Layer

**Revenue converts activity into system-level capital.**

Each interaction with liquidity generates fees. These fees are systematically distributed across three outcomes: reduction of circulating supply, reinforcement of liquidity providers, and accumulation into the balance sheet.

| Allocation | Function | System Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Burn | Supply reduction | Increases scarcity |
| LP Distribution | Liquidity reinforcement | Sustains market depth |
| Balance Sheet | Capital accumulation | Expands system capacity |

Revenue generation is continuous and directly proportional to activity.

{% hint style="success" %}
**System property**

All measurable activity produces system-level revenue.
{% endhint %}

This ensures that all usage contributes to system growth.

Revenue is generated by [Activity Layer](activity-layer.md), reflected in [Fee System](../mechanisms/fee-system.md), and accumulated into [Balance Sheet](balance-sheet.md).
