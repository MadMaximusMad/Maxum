# <img src="/icons/tokenomics.svg" width="18"/> Capital & Tokenomics

Maxum’s tokenomics are built on a single principle: **supply must be aligned with real, productive capital**.

Rather than relying on fixed supply narratives or uncontrolled emissions, MAX uses an adaptive model where expansion is tied directly to treasury growth and system activity. Every token in circulation is supported by capital the protocol has already captured.

This ensures that growth is not artificial — it is earned.

## The MAX Token

MAX is the base unit of value within the Maxum ecosystem.

It functions as:

* a settlement asset across applications
* a unit of account for liquidity and markets
* a mechanism for bonding and staking
* a governance asset

As the ecosystem expands, demand for MAX scales with usage, linking the token directly to economic activity within the system.

## Treasury-Backed Supply

Maxum does not expand supply arbitrarily.

Instead, circulating MAX is supported by treasury-backed value generated through:

* trading fees
* bonding inflows
* ecosystem revenue

As the treasury grows, the protocol gains the ability to expand supply in a controlled manner. When growth slows, expansion naturally reduces.

## Elastic Supply Model

MAX operates with an elastic supply model that allows the system to grow alongside its treasury.

When value is captured and backing increases, new tokens can be minted to reward long-term participants, support ecosystem expansion, and reinforce liquidity. Because supply growth is tied to treasury expansion, it remains structurally supported.

> [!TIP]
> Maxum expands supply only after value has been created, captured, and reinforced within the system.
