# Growth Model

**System growth is driven by capital formation and activity, not emissions.**

Maxum expands as capital formation increases and activity grows. New participants contribute capital, which is deployed into liquidity, enabling further activity and revenue generation.

The referral system distributes a portion of yield from the balance sheet to participants who onboard new users. This distribution is sourced from existing capital rather than new supply, ensuring that growth does not introduce dilution.

{% hint style="success" %}
**System dynamic**

Growth is internally funded and structurally aligned with the system loop.
{% endhint %}

As participation increases, capital expands, liquidity deepens, activity accelerates, and the balance sheet grows.

The capital basis of this process begins in [Capital Formation](../capital-flow/README.md) and returns through [System Expansion](system-expansion.md).
