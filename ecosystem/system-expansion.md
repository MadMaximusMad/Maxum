# System Expansion

**System expansion emerges from liquidity concentration and activity routing.**

As liquidity is deployed across markets, trading activity increasingly routes through MAX-based pairs. This concentration strengthens liquidity depth and reinforces market structure around the system.

Over time, this leads to increased transaction routing, higher fee generation, and accelerated balance sheet growth.

{% hint style="success" %}
**Emergent property**

Expansion is not imposed. It results from repeated usage and liquidity concentration.
{% endhint %}

System expansion depends on [Capital Deployment](../capital-flow/capital-deployment.md), is observed through [Activity Layer](../capital-flow/activity-layer.md), and compounds through [Balance Sheet](../capital-flow/balance-sheet.md).
