# Execution Constraints

**All governance actions are subject to enforced delay and deterministic execution.**

Once a proposal is approved, a fixed timelock is applied before execution. This delay ensures that participants have the opportunity to respond to upcoming changes.

Execution proceeds automatically after the timelock expires, without discretionary intervention.

{% hint style="success" %}
**Key property**

Governance is predictable, transparent, and non-discretionary.
{% endhint %}

This preserves system stability while allowing controlled adaptation over time.

Execution constraints complete the governance structure introduced in [Governance System](README.md) and bound the effects described in [Parameter Control](parameter-control.md).
