# <img src="/icons/mechanisms.svg" width="18"/> Fee & Burn

Every taxable buy and sell of MAX contributes to the protocol through a fee-on-transfer model. That fee is split across three destinations: burn, liquidity incentives, and treasury.

The burn component permanently removes tokens from circulation. The liquidity component deepens markets. The treasury component strengthens long-term capital formation. Each part serves a different function, but all three reinforce the same system.

![Fee Split](/diagrams/fee-split.svg)

## Why This Matters

The fee model allows Maxum to capture value from activity in real time. Even in periods where supply expansion slows, the system can still create treasury growth, support liquidity, and reduce circulating supply.

> [!TIP]
> Maxum’s fee model turns trading volume into deflation, market support, and treasury growth at the same time.
