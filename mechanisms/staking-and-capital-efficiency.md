# Staking & Capital Efficiency

**Staking introduces temporal structure to capital and improves system efficiency.**

Participants lock MAX for defined durations, reducing circulating supply while maintaining exposure to system growth. This reduces immediately available supply and concentrates activity through the remaining liquid base.

As a result, transaction throughput is expressed more efficiently, increasing the effectiveness of the fee mechanism and accelerating balance sheet growth.

Longer commitment durations increase capital retention, while shorter-term activity contributes to fee generation.

{% hint style="info" %}
**System effect**

Staking reduces circulating supply while preserving exposure to system growth.
{% endhint %}

The system dynamically reflects participant behavior, balancing long-term retention with short-term throughput.

This mechanism interacts with [Supply Model](README.md), increases the efficiency of [Fee System](fee-system.md), and strengthens the full [System Loop](../system/system-loop.md).
