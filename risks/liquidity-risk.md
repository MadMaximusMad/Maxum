# Liquidity Risk

**Liquidity is actively deployed and subject to redistribution.**

Under stress conditions, liquidity may shift across markets, affecting depth and execution conditions. This may impact price stability, slippage, and trade efficiency.

Liquidity risk is inherent to deployed capital systems.

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Interpretation**

Liquidity depth may vary materially under changing market conditions.
{% endhint %}
