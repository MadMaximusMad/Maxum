# Market Risk

**Market conditions affect pricing independently of system structure.**

Price dynamics are influenced by liquidity distribution, trading behavior, and external market conditions. Even with balance sheet backing, volatility may occur.

System state and market perception are not always perfectly aligned.

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Interpretation**

Balance sheet backing does not eliminate external market volatility.
{% endhint %}
