# Regulatory Risk

**Regulatory frameworks for on-chain systems are evolving.**

Jurisdictional changes may affect system accessibility, participation, and integration with external infrastructure. Participants are responsible for compliance within their respective regions.

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Constraint**

Regulatory developments may alter who can access or interact with the system and under what conditions.
{% endhint %}

This risk sits outside the internal mechanics of the [System Loop](../system/system-loop.md) but may affect participation in [Growth Model](../ecosystem/README.md) and the practical operation of all system components.
