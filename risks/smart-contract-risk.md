# Smart Contract Risk

**Smart contracts introduce execution risk.**

Despite testing and audits, vulnerabilities may exist within contract logic or integrations. These risks may affect capital custody, liquidity deployment, or transaction execution.

Absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Constraint**

Undiscovered vulnerabilities may exist despite comprehensive testing and review.
{% endhint %}

This risk is mitigated by the controls described in [Security Architecture](../system-controls/security-architecture.md) and affects the operational layers described in [Capital Deployment](../capital-flow/capital-deployment.md) and [Fee System](../mechanisms/fee-system.md).
