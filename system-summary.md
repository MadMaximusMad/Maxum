# System Summary

Maxum operates as a continuous capital system in which liquidity, activity, and revenue are interconnected.

Capital is deployed into liquidity. Liquidity enables markets. Markets generate activity. Activity produces revenue. Revenue is returned to the system and redeployed.

This loop defines how the system functions and how it grows.

As liquidity expands across applications, MAX becomes the central asset through which markets operate. Its role emerges from usage, becoming more prominent as it is integrated across the system.

Over time, this establishes Maxum as a foundational layer for on-chain economies, supporting applications and markets that depend on deep and reliable liquidity.
